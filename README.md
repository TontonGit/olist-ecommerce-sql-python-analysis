# 📊 Olist E-commerce Data Analysis (SQL & Python)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset** to uncover key business insights related to:

- Revenue distribution  
- Customer behavior  
- Business growth trends  
- Customer segmentation  

The analysis combines **SQL** for data extraction and **Python** for data analysis and visualization, demonstrating an end-to-end analytics workflow.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

This analysis focuses on answering the following questions:

- Where does revenue come from?  
- How has the business grown over time?  
- Who are the customers?  
- Do customers return and make repeat purchases?  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **SQL (MySQL)** → Data extraction and transformation  
- **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)** → Data analysis and visualization  
- **Jupyter Notebook** → Development environment

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Revenue is geographically concentrated in a few key states  
- The business experienced rapid growth followed by stabilization  
- The customer base is dominated by low-engagement users  
- Customer retention is a major opportunity for improvement  

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

- Diversify geographic expansion to reduce regional dependency  
- Investigate revenue spikes to identify repeatable growth drivers  
- Focus on customer retention to improve long-term profitability  
- Leverage high-value customers through targeted engagement
